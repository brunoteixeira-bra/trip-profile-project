Atacama Desert

The Atacama Desert in northern Chile is one of the driest places on Earth, with stunning landscapes of salt flats, volcanoes, and geysers. Its unique scenery makes it a paradise for stargazing and adventure lovers.

Tourist attractions

  • Valle de la Luna
